Roughly towards the end of November in 2022 in Sydney, students across Hurlstone Agricultural High School developed shared memories of a man named Ric Morante (interestingly unanimously nicknamed Doc Roc) teaching regularly at the school.

All students swear that they have not seen the man since. This specific image is the only known image of "Doc Roc" hand drawn by a senior shortly before graduation. In more than one occasion, the senior has memories of this man giving them advice on their career. The hand drawn image was photographed by another student whom felt a sense of familiarity to the image as well

The image lay forgotten in the gallery of the student until by chance, their friends saw the image and also claimed to have memories of the man. Since then, students across various grades have claimed to recognise the face and all have memories of "Doc Roc" teaching at HAHS. Many students claim to have seen him regularly in the hallways. To this date, at least 200 students have claimed to have seen as THIS MAN.

From Feburary 2020 until today, at least 450 1350 people have claimed they have seen this man in their dreams, in many places all over the world: COLA, Steam Lawn, Hall, Science rooms, Math corridor, Library, Volleyball courts etc.

At the moment there is no ascertained relation or common trait among the people that have memories of seeing this man. Moreover, "Doc Roc" or any man resembling him has not been seen across the school by any student or teacher. Authorities are yet to make a comment on this phenomenon.

The aim of this website is:
– to help those who have seen this man before, and to foster communication among them;
– to understand who this man is and why he appears in an apparently pattern-less array of situations in the dreams of such diverse human subjects.
